Lover

Lover is a pop-related album. In this album, Taylor Swift wrote songs about her being really happy. She was embracing herself more and more. In some songs, she was also talking about certain public issues that needed to be adressed like homophobia in You Need to Calm Down and she was also addressing gender inequality in her song The Man. Most of all, I love Cruel Summer and Lover since it has this bubbly vibe like my personality.
